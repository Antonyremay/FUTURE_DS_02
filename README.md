# FUTURE_DS_02
Customer retention and churn analysis for a subscription-based business using Python, including cohort analysis, churn trends, customer lifetime insights, and actionable recommendations.
# Customer Retention & Churn Analysis

## Business Objective
Explain churn problem in simple business language

## Dataset
Telco Customer Churn (Kaggle)

## Key KPIs
- Overall Churn Rate
- Avg Tenure of Churned Customers

## Key Insights
- Month-to-month customers churn the most
- Early tenure is the highest risk period
- High monthly charges increase churn

## Recommendations
- Incentivize long-term contracts
- Improve onboarding in first 3 months
- Offer pricing flexibility

## Tools Used
Python, Pandas, Matplotlib
